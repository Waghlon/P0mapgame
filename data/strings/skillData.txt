Skill Names	Skill Description
Sketching	Sketching allows you to work both with a pencil and digitally, to illustrate your ideas before working on it.
Programming	Programming is how you tell the computer to do what you want it to do. At AAU, languages as Java and Processing is commonly used. 
Modelling	Modelling is the construction of digital 3D models, which are used in both movies, video games and other projects.
Physical Design	Physical design is the understanding of how a project will work in real life, in terms of hardware and construction.
Interaction Design	Interaction design is knowing how users think and how they will interact with your project.
Animation	Animation consists of making 2D and 3D graphics move in a realistic and beautiful way.
Sound Design	Sound design is the understanding of how to use sounds, how to process audio and using sound sensor technology.
